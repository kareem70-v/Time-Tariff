Why Time Tariff?

Pay for Time, Not for Coffee

In a traditional café, you’re paying for a product. At Time Tariff, you’re investing in an environment. Once you're on the clock, the refreshments are on us.

The Perk: Unlimited premium tea, coffee, and snacks.

A "No-Pressure" Zone

Forget the guilt of sitting with a single cup for hours. Whether you're writing your next novel or prepping a data report, the table is yours as long as you're checked in.

The Perk: Guaranteed seating with power outlets.

Built for Productivity

Traditional cafés are loud and distracting. We curate an atmosphere designed for deep work, creative brainstorming, and quiet reading.

The Perk: Business-grade high-speed Wi-Fi.

Your Food, Your Rules

Tired of expensive café sandwiches? We are one of the few places where you can bring your own tiffin or order a delivery right to your table. We provide the space; you choose the menu.

The Perk: Microwave access and a "Bring Your Own Food" friendly policy.

About Me and Idea

I am a BSc. IT student. In 2026, I learned about the Anti-Cafe concept from a friend and immediately recognized its potential as a great business opportunity. After conducting extensive research, I developed a vision for my own space; however, the original concept was too vast and exceeded my budget.
To make the project more feasible, I modified the plan by removing certain high-cost elements. To ensure the venue remains attractive and unique, I integrated a new idea: a dedicated Quiet Zone Area.
